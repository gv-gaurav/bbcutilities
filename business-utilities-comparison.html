What are the Business Energy Tariffs Available in 2025?

Fixed-Rate Tariffs – Stability and Budget Control

A fixed-rate energy tariff secures your unit rate and standing charge for a set period — typically between 1 and 5 years. This option is ideal for businesses looking for price certainty and predictable monthly bills, especially in times of fluctuating energy prices. ✅ Best for Great for steady budgeting and long-term goals

Variable Rate Tariffs – Flexibility with Market Prices

Variable tariffs allow your energy rates to move in line with wholesale market prices. While this can result in lower bills when prices drop, it also exposes your business to potential price hikes. ✅ Ideal for businesses with higher risk tolerance or short-term energy needs.

Rollover Tariffs – Auto-Renewed but Costly

If you don’t renegotiate or switch your energy plan before your contract ends, you may be placed on a rollover tariff. These are more expensive and gets you locked for a short or fixed period.

⚠️ Generally not cost-effective – avoid by reviewing your contract regularly.

Green Energy Tariffs – Power Your Business Sustainably

With a growing focus on sustainability, green electricity tariffs have become a popular choice. These plans source energy from renewable providers such as wind or solar, allowing your business to reduce its carbon footprint. ✅ Perfect for businesses committed to ESG or net-zero goals.

Pass-Through Tariffs – Greater Control for Engaged Businesses

A pass-through tariff These tariffs split your energy bill into fixed wholesale rates and separate variable charges such as network costs and environmental levies. They're a smart fit for businesses that keep a close eye on their usage or want clearer insight into where their energy spend is going. ✅ Best for medium to large businesses that monitor and adjust consumption.

Deemed Rate Tariffs – The Most Expensive Option

Businesses without an agreed contract — such as after moving into new premises — are usually placed on deemed rates. These are some of the highest-priced tariffs in the market and should be avoided where possible. ⚠️ Secure a proper contract immediately to avoid excessive charges.

Flexible Procurement Tariffs – For High-Energy Users

Large organisations or multi-site businesses may benefit from flexible tariffs, which allow bulk energy purchasing at wholesale rates. This gives greater access to market timing, potentially bypassing retailer premiums. ✅ Trusted by 3000+ Business Owners with higher energy usage and looking for more control over their Utilities comparison.
